Read Me

Welcome to our site! Here are some instructions to get you started:

  • Not a user? : Use Django administration site to register your self as a user.
  • Login: Use your credentials to log in to the site.
  • Home: View and manage your personal blog posts space where you can create a new post, view your post, update your post, delete your post.
  • Create post: Where you can crate your own post by giving a personalized title, the content and the status(Draft/Update) of you post.
  • Post: Where you can see yours and other users posts in there.
  • Logout: Click on the logout button to sign out.
